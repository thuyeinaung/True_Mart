POS - Point of sales Membership Management Odoo Apps

Point of sales Membership Management Odoo Apps allows your cutomers to provide POS Membership Card for provide Discount on their orders to build Loyalty via Discount Card for Members. Seller can create various membership categories with some associated discounts. POS Membership cards are created and assigned to the customers. POS Customers can use their membership cards to avail discount on their orders. Seller can apply membership card by scanning membership card barcode in POS screen on Odoo.
